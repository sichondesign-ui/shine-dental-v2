Meet Your Doctor

Meet Dr. Vaishali Patel

A Dentist Who Leads With Heart

With 14 years of experience, Dr. Vaishali Patel has built a reputation for clinical excellence and the kind of compassionate care that stays with you. She has trained and coached fellow dentists, perfected the art of painless procedures, and brings a precision and passion to her work that patients feel from their very first visit.

Her love for children runs especially deep. She has a true gift for making young patients feel safe, comfortable, and genuinely proud of themselves. Outside the office, Dr. Patel volunteers with the Sharing Smiles program, providing free care to uninsured children and adults, and proudly supports Special Olympics Virginia.

She Knows This Community

Dr. Patel has practiced nearby for years. She chose this location because she genuinely cares about the people who live here, and it shows in every appointment.

Your Comfort Is Her Priority

No rushing. No judgment. Just gentle, thoughtful care tailored to you, the way she'd want her own family treated.
